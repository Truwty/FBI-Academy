About the Academy

At the FBI Academy, you will undergo intense training to become part of an elite team of federal agents. Our program is designed to prepare recruits for various real-world operations and tactical missions.

Choose Your Role

Specialise in a variety of roles within the FBI, each with unique missions and responsibilities.

FBI Agent

Become a part of the Federal Bureau of Investigation, handling criminal investigations, counterintelligence, and law enforcement operations.

SWAT Officer

Join the Special Weapons and Tactics team, handling high-risk operations, hostage rescues, and tactical assaults.

Investigator

Work as a specialist in criminal investigations, gathering evidence, and solving cases that require meticulous attention to detail.

Counterterrorism Officer

Specialized in neutralizing terrorism threats and operations.
